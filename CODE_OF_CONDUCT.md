# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Farsi UI is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive community for all members, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone and will not tolerate any form of discrimination or abuse.

---

## Our Standards

### Positive Behaviors

We encourage community members to:

- **Be respectful** and kind in all interactions
- **Be inclusive** and welcoming to people from diverse backgrounds
- **Be collaborative** and constructive in feedback
- **Be professional** and courteous
- **Be open** to different perspectives and ideas
- **Acknowledge mistakes** and learn from feedback
- **Focus on criticism** of ideas, not people

### Unacceptable Behaviors

The following behaviors are unacceptable in our community:

- Harassment, intimidation, or bullying
- Discriminatory comments or actions based on protected characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Insults, attacks, or derogatory comments
- Spam, flooding, or disruptive behavior
- Threats of violence or harmful actions
- Trolling or intentionally provoking others
- Any other conduct that violates laws or harms the community

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All community spaces (GitHub, discussions, social media, events)
- Official project spaces and communications
- Public spaces when representing the project
- Private communications related to project matters

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. **Emailing**: conduct@farsi-ui.example.com (replace with actual email)
2. **Opening an issue**: Only for non-sensitive matters
3. **Direct message**: To a project maintainer (for sensitive issues)

Please include:
- Description of the incident
- People involved
- Any relevant context
- Screenshots or logs (if applicable)

**All reports will be handled with confidentiality and care.**

### Investigation

When a report is made:

1. Project maintainers will review the report
2. They will investigate the situation
3. They will work to resolve the issue fairly
4. They will follow up with all parties involved

### Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

1. **First violation**: Warning and discussion
2. **Repeated violations**: Temporary ban from the community
3. **Severe or persistent violations**: Permanent ban from the community

The severity of the response will be proportional to the violation and any pattern of behavior.
